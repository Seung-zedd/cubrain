## What's New?

We've focused on the "feel" of Cubrain in this update. By refining the vertical rhythm and adding high-end animations, the landing page now feels more like a premium storytelling experience.

### 🧘‍♂️ Improved Visual Rhythm

- **Spacious Layout**: We've increased the vertical padding between all major sections. This "breathable" design helps you focus on one concept at a time without feeling overwhelmed by information.
- **Logical Flow Optimization**: The page now follows a smoother conversion funnel: Proof first, then Value, then Interaction.

### 🪄 Feature Grid "Wave" Animation

- **Staggered Entrance**: The Feature Grid now elegantly floats into view as you scroll.
- **Premium Easing**: We've implemented a custom `cubic-bezier` easing function for a smooth, high-end deceleration effect.
- **Intentional Triggering**: The animation now waits until at least 50% of the grid is visible, ensuring the "wow" moment happens exactly when you're looking at it.

### 🛠️ UX & Redirection Polish

- **Context-Aware Redirection**: Clicking "Upgrade" buttons now intelligently redirects you to the Settings page after login, rather than the general Library, so you can manage your plan immediately.
- **Subtle Background Depth**: Added a radial gradient behind the feature section to create a soft visual anchor and separate it from the demos above.

#### **🚀 Why This Matters?**

Software isn't just about features; it's about the feeling of using it. This update ensures that your first interaction with Cubrain is as smooth and professional as the flashcards it generates.
