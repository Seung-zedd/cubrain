## What's New?

This milestone update brings a comprehensive **UI Renovation** to Cubrain, unifying our visual language and elevating the overall "Glassmorphism & Focus" experience.

### 🍱 UI Renovation & Aesthetic Sync

We've meticulously updated the entire interface to align with our premium design tokens:

- **Enhanced Glassmorphism**: Universal application of the Cubrain glass effect—`bg-white/10`, `backdrop-blur-md`, and subtle `border-white/20`.
- **Gold Accent Refinement**: Standardized all primary action elements and highlights to **Cubrain Gold (#FFC107)** for better visual hierarchy.
- **Micro-interaction Polish**: Added subtle hover states and transitions to interactive cards and buttons, making the app feel alive and responsive.

### 💎 Premium Design Consistency

- **Dark Mode Optimization**: Deepened background contrasts for a more immersive Zinc-900 experience.
- **Typography Standardized**: Unified font weights and sizes across the marketing and application layers.
- **Component Harmony**: Re-aligned the `LaunchBanner` and other layout elements to the new design system for a seamless flow.

---

#### **🚀 Why This Matters?**

Version 1.5.0 marks a significant shift from "functional" to "premium". This UI renovation ensures that every pixel of Cubrain reflects the precision and quality of the study tools it provides.
