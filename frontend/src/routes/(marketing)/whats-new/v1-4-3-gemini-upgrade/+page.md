## What's New?

We've upgraded the brain of Cubrain! This update migrates our backend AI models to the latest **Gemini 2.5 Flash**, ensuring your study materials are generated with state-of-the-art speed and intelligence.

### 🧠 Gemini 2.5 Flash Upgrade

We've moved away from the 2.0 series to embrace the next generation of Google's "workhorse" model.

- **Future-Proofing**: By migrating to Gemini 2.5 Flash, we've ensured continued support and performance improvements ahead of the 2.0 series deprecation.
- **Enhanced Reasoning**: Experience more accurate and nuanced flashcard generation, especially for complex technical and scientific documents.
- **Optimized Performance**: Gemini 2.5 Flash provides the perfect balance of speed and intelligence, keeping your PDF processing times lightning-fast.

### ⚙️ Backend Refinements

- **Environment Sync**: Updated all configurations (Local, Dev, Prod, and Test) to use the new model consistently.
- **Test Suite Validation**: Verified the integration with our full backend test suite to ensure stability during the transition.

---

#### **🚀 Why Gemini 2.5 Flash?**

Google's Gemini 2.5 Flash is designed for advanced reasoning, coding, and scientific tasks. By integrating this model, Cubrain stays at the cutting edge of AI-powered education, providing you with the most reliable study tools possible.
